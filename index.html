About Chef Amar Padge

I’m Chef Amar Padage, a passionate culinary professional from Maharashtra with a degree from Chef's Kitchen Institute of Culinary Arts and Hotel Management, Kolhapur. During my academic journey, I actively participated in campus events and advanced kitchen practicals that strengthened my culinary foundation.

I’ve worked in 150+ banquets, completed an international internship in Mauritius, where I trained in breakfast, pastry, hot kitchen, and cold kitchen sections, gaining hands-on experience across multiple departments. I also took part in competitions like Art on Plate and the Nestlé Food Event, and attended a live cooking demo by Chef Dev Kasalkar, Rational India’s combi oven specialist.

Beyond the kitchen, I supported my college’s 2025–2026 academic year opening event, assisting with guest management, documentation, and orientation. With a solid understanding of HACCP food safety standards, I bring hygiene, discipline, and creativity to every dish I prepare.
